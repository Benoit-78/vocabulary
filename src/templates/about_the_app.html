Welcome to my vocabulary application!

This application allows you to review words in a foreign language.

For instance, each time you discover a new word, add it to the app along with its translation.

The app will then ask you, say, 10 random words each day.

As you progress, the words you retain well will be archived, while those you have difficulties with will be revisited more frequently.

And in the end, all of these words will be in your memory!

Happy learning!

Do NOT store any confidential data in this app. Data storage AND data exchanges are currently NOT secure.
