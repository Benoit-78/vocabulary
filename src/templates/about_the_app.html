Welcome to my vocabulary application!

This application allows you to save and review words in a foreign language at your own pace.

For instance, whenever you come across a new word while watching a series, simply add it to the app, along with its translation.

The app will then present you with, say, 10 random words each day.

As you progress, the words you excel at will be archived, while those you find challenging will be revisited more frequently.

Happy learning!
